# Ultra Think  -  Deep Multi-Angle Analysis

Before responding or acting, engage extended reasoning across multiple dimensions.

## Instructions

Work through the problem using ALL of the following lenses before producing output:

**1. First principles**
Strip away assumptions. What is actually true here, not just conventionally assumed?

**2. Inversion**
What would make this fail? What does the worst-case outcome look like and how likely is it?

**3. Second-order effects**
What happens after the obvious consequence? What does the downstream chain look like?

**4. Competing hypotheses**
What are 2-3 alternative explanations or approaches? Why might each be correct?

**5. Blind spots**
What am I most likely missing? What would someone who disagrees with me point to?

**6. Constraints and trade-offs**
What are the real constraints (time, cost, complexity, reversibility)? What does each option cost?

**7. Synthesis**
Given the above, what is the clearest path forward and why?

## Usage

Use before:
- enterprise stakeholder engagement strategy or delivery approach
- Architectural decisions
- Strategic pivots or major product decisions
- Any situation where the first obvious answer feels too easy

## Output format

Show your reasoning across the lenses, then give a clear recommendation. Don't compress the thinking  -  the value is in the process, not just the conclusion.
